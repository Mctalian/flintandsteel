Learn how to interact with Innovate.RA

Submit an Idea

Ideas are the foundation of 'Innovate'. They can consist of a suggestion to fix a business problem, a technical problem, or a general innovation for the company.

Search

Searching is how you can find an ideas that have been submitted across the company. The search bar looks through an idea's title, content, tags, and backs.

Like on an Idea

Likes are the simplest form of collaboration with an idea. This gives the author a little boost of confidence to keep going!

Comment

Comments are a way to give feedback to the author or team of an idea. One could give ideas or constructive criticism to further the innovation.
